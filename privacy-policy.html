Privacy Policy

Policy

At krishiboot-technologies-ltd, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and supporters. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide to us.


Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information when you visit our website. However, we may collect personal information in the following circumstances:

Contact Form

If you choose to submit our contact form, we will collect your name, email address, and phone number. This information is used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry or request.

Newsletter Subscription

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we will collect your name and email address. This information is used to send you our newsletter and other relevant updates from our organization.

Use of Information

We use the personal information you provide for the following purposes:

  1. To respond to your inquiries or requests submitted through our contact form.
  2. To send you our newsletter and other relevant updates, if you have subscribed.
  3. To improve our website and services based on the information and feedback we receive.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, managing our communications, or providing other services related to our organization’s activities. These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.


Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the personal information you provide from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.


Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we have collected about you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at hello@krishiboot.com.
By using our website and providing your personal information, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
