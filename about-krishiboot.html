About Us

Revolutionizing Agriculture

Krishiboot is an Indian AgriTech startup developing autonomous, AI-powered robots to solve critical challenges in modern farming. Our modular robotic platform is designed for precision spraying, weeding, and sowing — enabling higher productivity, lower input usage, and reduced labor dependency.

Built for Indian farm conditions, Krishiboot blends deep-tech innovation with real-world practicality. We are focused on creating scalable, affordable automation solutions that can transform agriculture at the grassroots level. Our vision is to lead the agricultural robotics revolution in India — empowering farmers and reshaping the future of food production.

Our Vision

Revolutionizing Agriculture Through Automated AI Powered Robots.

Our Mission

To empower Indian farmers with intelligent, affordable, and adaptive robotic solutions that reduce labor burden, optimize input use, and increase productivity — making precision farming accessible at scale.
