About Us

Growing up in a small village, farming wasn’t just work — it was a way of life. Watching my family, especially my uncle, battle the harsh realities of agriculture, I saw firsthand the challenges farmers face every day — long hours, rising costs, and unpredictable results.

Years later, after graduating from IIT Bombay with a passion for robotics and innovation, one thing was clear: the real impact of technology should be felt where it's needed most — in the fields that feed us.

Krishiboot was born from that belief. It's not about building futuristic machines for show — it's about creating reliable, intelligent helpers that work shoulder-to-shoulder with farmers. With Krishiboot, we are bringing precision, affordability, and empowerment directly to India's farms — where technology becomes a true partner in growth.

Our Vision

To become India’s Revolutionary robotics company — transforming the future of farming through the power of AI and robotics.

Our Mission

To empower Indian farmers with intelligent, affordable, and adaptive robotic solutions that reduce labor burden, optimize input use, and increase productivity — making precision farming accessible at scale.
