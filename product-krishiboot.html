Meet Agriyan

AI-Automated Agric Bot

Our new robot is designed with a modular architecture, allowing easy attachment and switching of different farming implements like sprayers, weeders, and sowers — offering flexibility for multiple tasks in one platform.

The new Krishiboot-robot "Agriyan", is built to adapt to various row spacings and is also equipped with advanced navigation and obstacle-sensing capabilities for india farms.
