About Us

Tackling Labor-Intensive Challenges in Agriculture

We are an AgriTech startup based in India, on a mission to revolutionize farming through autonomous robotics and artificial intelligence. Our goal is to tackle labor-intensive challenges in agriculture and bring precision, efficiency, and scalability to farming practices.

With a strong focus on real-world application, we are developing modular autonomous robots capable of tasks such as precision spraying, precision weeding, and precision sowing, all tailored to the diverse needs of Indian farms. Driven by a passionate core team, we are building the future of farming — one intelligent robot at a time.

Agriyan Key Features

Modular Structure

Our robot is designed with a modular architecture, allowing easy integration of multiple attachments for various farming operations like spraying, weeding, and sowing.

Adjustable for different crop row spacing

The system can be configured to adapt to different crop row widths, making it suitable for a wide range of crops commonly grown in Indian farms.

Autonomous Day night Operation

Equipped with advanced sensors and AI navigation, the robot can operate reliably both during the day and at night, maximizing productivity across longer working hours.
